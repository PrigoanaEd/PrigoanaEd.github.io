The Golden Temple

The Golden Temple is a building covered in gold located in India. In this Blog Post, I will share some facts about it, key features, and justify why Sikhs go there on pilgirmage!

Facts

> The Golden Temple is on an island.
> The Golden Temple is open to all worshippers.
> The Golden Temple is a gurdwara.
> The Golden Temple dates back to the 1500's.
> The Golden Temple houses the Guru Granth Sahib.

Key Features

> The Golden Temple is famous for its big, golden dome.
> The Golden Temple's architecture resembles that of buildings found in the Indian Subcontinent.
> Guru Arjan rather decided to build the shrine lower than the surroundings so that devotees would have to walk downwards to reach it.
> The floral designs on the marble panels of the walls around the sanctum are Arabesque.
> The arches include verses from the Sikh scripture in gold letters.
