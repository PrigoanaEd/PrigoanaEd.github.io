To use AltStore to install a modded Spotify client to your iPhone/iPad, click this, then download and run it. After that, connect your device to your PC with a usb-lightning cable, and run AltServer. An icon will appear on the right side of your taskbar, click on that and select install altstore -> (your device). follow the on-screen instructions, and wait until AltStore installs. Then, go here and download the .ipa file. In iTunes, click on the phone icon. under file sharing, select any app. drag the .ipa file there. after, on your device, open AltStore, then under my apps, click the + icon, and navigate to the IPA file. select it, and install it. After, go here to select and add songs to a playlist.
