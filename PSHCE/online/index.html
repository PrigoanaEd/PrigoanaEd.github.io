Is it better to meet people Online or Offline?

Let's start with the pros and cons of either side.

Offline Pros
Online Pros
- You can see who you actually meet - You can meet people interested in more niche topics
- You can connect better - You can meet more people with similar interests
- You can hang out in real life - You can meet people with similar experiences 
Offline Cons
Online Cons
- People might try taking advantage of you - Predators could be lurking
- You can't find nearly as much people as you can online - You might encounter some weird people
- The people you meet might not share interests - You can never truly know who the other person is

In my opinion, People you meet both ways can be nice, good people. But you have to be more careful online, as people could try to mask who they really are. In general, it doesn't really matter as long as you use common sense.
