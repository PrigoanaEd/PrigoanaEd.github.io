Contributing to BikeBorb's Shop!


Prequisites


•Complete the form, you will get a ping telling you you have been accepted. If some time passes and you still don't have acces, provide an admin your gmail adress so you can ba manually added.

•Download this modified version of NXFileViewer that verifies compressed files (courtesy of BestPig) (requires updated switch keys to be in program directory, get them from your device or from here)

•OPTIONAL BUT PREFERRED: Download NSZ by nicoboss from here to compress all uploads (saves bandwidth, avoids files from being inaccessible after reaching bandwidth quota) (requires switch keys in the /nsz/ directory, get from your device or from here)
